Solution Area

Call Center and Help Desk

Partner Type

Solution ISV

Based In

San Francisco, CA

NewVoiceMedia is the leading provider of true cloud contact center and voice solutions for Salesforce that enables both Sales & Service departments to deliver a personalized customer experience across phone, email, and chat (inbound and outbound). Sales organizations can maximize revenue potential by automating campaign follow up, increasing lead conversion and empowering field reps to be more productive from their mobile devices. Once the sale is made, service agents are then empowered to deliver a personalized, consistent customer service experience with intelligent case routing and call recordings to drive their productivity. NewVoiceMedia’s technology is tailored specifically for the Salesforce environment but agnostic when it comes to the phone carrier. A single, cloud contact center infrastructure is created when a customer chooses salesforce.com for CRM, NewVoiceMedia for CTI, and Twilio for phone. NewVoiceMedia is the highest rated CTI app on the Salesforce AppExchange.
