Commerce Communications

Instant notifications and calls to stay connected with customers at every step of a transaction.

Talk to an Expert

Whether you’re in e-commerce or the sharing economy, it’s important to keep customers informed and connect them with the right person when things go wrong. The Twilio API gives you a way to connect just that using text, chat, calls, and video.

What Can You Build With Twilio?

  • Masked Phone Numbers

    Connect people without sharing personal numbers. Bridge their texts and calls through an intermediate Twilio phone number.

    Learn more

  • Arrival Alerts

    Coordinate arrivals and deliveries with texts or calls, so neither you nor your customer are left waiting.

    Learn more

  • Order Notifications

    Provide order status by sending text confirmations and tracking updates.

  • SMS Sales

    Create a private text channel that customers can use to contact sales reps and make purchases.

  • Account Notifications

    Send text and voice alerts about suspicious transactions and unexpected changes that require immediate attention.
