Appointment Reminders

Reduce no-shows by sending confirmation messages from CRM and reservation systems.

  • Better than email

    Deliver reminders the way your customers want. Use SMS to get a 5X higher open rate than email, with 90% read in 4 minutes.

  • Stop manual calling

    Use your software to automatically notify customers of upcoming appointments. Two-way SMS and voice prompts give customers the option to confirm or reschedule.

  • Fill Open Slots

    Fill your calendar when there are cancellations. Notify and book waitlisted customers via automated texts and voice calls.

Arkansas Children's Hospital reduces no-shows by 20 per day via voice and SMS reminders.

Why Add Appointment Reminders with Twilio?

  • Fast integration

    Create intelligent reminders with integration to CRM and reservation systems. The Twilio API lets you add notification workflows with the programming language you know.

  • Pay-as-you-go

    Pay for what you use, not what you might use. With the Twilio API you pay on a per-message and a per-minute basis. No subscriptions, contracts, or commitments.

  • Voice fallback

    Start with a text reminder and fallback to an automated voice call if the message isn’t delivered. Twilio offers a message delivery status API so you can programmatically initiate a voice call.

  • Two-way interactions

    Let customers confirm, cancel, or reschedule by enabling two-way texting and voice prompts. The API gives you control to programmatically react to responses.

Handy added Appointment Reminders to notify customers of scheduled services. Customers can communicate directly with service professionals right from the reminder and without revealing personal numbers.
